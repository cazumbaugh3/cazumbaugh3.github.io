Rally: The 2A Community

Rally is a community for Second Amendment advocates to come together on iOS devices. Users are able to discuss anything and everything relating to the Second Amendment. The forum categories cover everything from different classes of firearms, current legislation, shooting ranges, and an umbrella category for everything else! Users can also view recent federal legislation that impacts all things Second Amendment right at their fingertips!
